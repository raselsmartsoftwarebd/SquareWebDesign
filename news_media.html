ABOUT US

SQUARE is founded on a vision to serve people with products and services that help them to live a better life. With a pioneering spirit, SQUARE is building multinational businesses having significant investments in different parts of the world. Burgeoning to multiple companies, the group has now businesses as diverse as healthcare, textiles, consumer goods, media, and information technology.
