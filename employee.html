Employee Wellbeing

Each member of SQUARE is our pride and the main driving force for any development

Happy Employee is a Good Employee

SQUARE is committed to performing purposeful activities to promote environmental awareness and help to mitigate climate changes that have a serious effect on our natural resources. Its focus is on water and energy conservation, Green House Gas reduction, and waste minimization in our factory operations. To ensure our sustainable environmental practices, SQUARE follows the 5R principles i.e., Reduce, Reuse, Refuse, Recycle, and Recovery. The following environmental initiatives define SQUARE’s approach towards creating a safe environment:

● Identifying the environmental aspects and impacts of our business activity.

● Developing and implementing measures to mitigate the environmental impact.

● Using resources as efficiently as possible.

● Ensuring compliance with relevant legislation and international standards.

● Raising environmental awareness among our employees.

● Minimizing the environmental impact due to business operations.

● Encouraging our customers to invest in environmentally friendly initiatives.

SQUARE promises to make a better and greener Bangladesh. Our approach to environmental management enables us to detect and avoid potential environmental impacts at an early stage. The environmental plans contain a clear set of objectives and concrete activities to be carried out as per the guideline set by the national regulatory bodies and ISO. Its manufacturing plants have instituted an internationally accepted Environmental Management System to restrict pollution activities.

● To implement a comprehensive and sustainable system for managing all environment-related activities.

● To raise environmental awareness in SQUARE and help to reduce the negative environmental impact of the organization's operations.

● To continuously improve the organization’s environmental performance.

● To integrate environmental protection as a corporate value into each organization’s philosophy.

● To raise awareness and the level of knowledge among employees and customers concerning environmental topics.

● To present the environmental performance and activities of the organization both internally and externally.

● SQUARE highly discourages launching products in plastic sachets. On the other hand, initiatives are ongoing to reduce plastic consumption, and recycling of plastic is encouraged to be more responsible towards the environment.

● We are placing incinerators in our factories to be more responsible towards the environment. The first unit is already installed at Rupshi Plant for use.

● Paper packs are under exploration for washing powder category products; this will reduce a significant of poly pack usage.

● Dr. Mubarak Ahmad Khan, who invented a biodegradable polybag known as Sonali Bag is also working with us to develop biodegradable raw materials for different products.

Pro-employee corporate business house

We are widely recognized nationally and internationally as a pro-employee corporate business house. Our dedication lies in health, education, and empowerment for our employees. The employees along with their family members are provided with health care facilities.

ABOUT US

With a pioneering spirit, SQUARE is building multinational businesses having significant investments in different parts of the world. Burgeoning to multiple companies, the group has now businesses as diverse as healthcare, textiles, consumer goods, media, and information technology.
